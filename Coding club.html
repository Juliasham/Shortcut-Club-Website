JavaScript Club

The JavaScript Club is a peer to peer learning club where participants have the chance to learn through hands-on sessions that will get them acquainted with JavaScript.
Every Tuesday from 16.30 to 18.30 at the Shortcut Lab.

For whom:

The JavaScript Club is open to everyone eager to learn and advance step by step each week. No matter if you have experience or not.

How:

Each session is comprised of discussions and peer learning where you can learn from experienced JavaScript coders.
Those who already have some knowledge on JavaScript can support/share/teach others.
We will also provide various workshops on Git, Azure, AWS. Their exact dates will be announced later in the event page and by email.

Registration:

Not required, but appreciate dropping a message about your participation to don@theshortcut.org

Note:

Remember to bring your laptop!

Girls'Coding Club

The Girls'Çoding Club is to give the opportunity to women to improve their coding knowledge. This is not a class but a club where participants get together and learn without teachers( except for special occasions).
Every Wednesday from 17.00 to 19.00 at the Shortcut Lab.

For whom:

The Girls'Çoding Club is open to anyone who supports women in tech.

How:

We use FreeCodeCamp curriculum to grow to knowledge and skills little by little. The aim is to get started with learning to code and then support each other to keep going. Each week we are working on different topics going step by step through HTML, HTML5, CSS, JavaScript. A Hackathon every 6 months is organised by the club participants to test how their skills have evolved and validate the learnings.

Registration:

Not required, but appreciate dropping a message about your participation to don@theshortcut.org

Note:

Remember to bring your laptop!
